IP Camera Live Streaming and Cloud Recording

WebRTC (Web Real-Time Communication) cloud based technology provides video player componant (HTML 5) can displaye videos on the various platforms. It can smoothly works on the Desktop, Tablet, Mobile and even on the various social sites including you tube, facebook, instagram, your own webpage.

As the Player is web browser based and easily integrate with all browsers (safari, google, mozila, bing) it connect with IP camera seamlessly via HTTTP / RTSP streaming protocol.

The streaming quality is HD, FHD and 4K depending upon the Camera, Network and the system used.

As it is based on unified network web so unlimited concurrent views can be possible without buffering.

Streams obtained are H.264, MPEG4, MJPEG, AVC.

 

 

The H.264 compressed videos can be downloded.

The H.264 compressed videos of daily time-lapse clips will be a just 2 min video clip.

WebRTC enabled browsers support media codecs like H.264 This skips the transcoding. Native WebRTC gets the raw frames from the device camera, then encodes it into browser- compatible formats and pass to the browser.
